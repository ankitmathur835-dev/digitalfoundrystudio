About

A narrow, intentional offering.

DigitalFoundry is a one-person studio with a simple goal: make what you already have look and feel more considered.

Instead of trying to do everything, I focus on a few specific pieces: logo cleanup, document summaries, upgraded headshots, and short-form content support. AI helps with speed; the value is in curation, editing, and polish.
