About

A narrow, intentional offering.

DigitalFoundry is a one-person studio focused on a small set of things done well: tightening what you already have instead of rebuilding everything from scratch.

Instead of offering every design service under the sun, I work on a few specific pieces: logos that are “almost there,” PDFs that are too long to revisit, photos that are nearly “profile ready,” and short clips that would land better with clean, readable captions.

Services

Where I can help.

Defined offerings that are easy to say yes to. You send what you have; I send back something more considered and ready to use.
