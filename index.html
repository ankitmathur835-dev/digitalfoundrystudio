About

A narrow, intentional offering.

DigitalFoundry is a one-person studio built around a simple idea: fewer services, done with more attention, for people who already have something to work with.

Rather than designing everything from zero, I focus on tightening what already exists: logos that are “almost there,” PDFs that are too long to revisit, photos that are good but not quite “profile ready,” and short clips that would land better with clean captions.
