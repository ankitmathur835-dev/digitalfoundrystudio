About

A narrow, intentional offering.

DigitalFoundry is a one-person studio focused on a small set of things done well: tightening what you already have instead of rebuilding everything from scratch.

Instead of offering every design service under the sun, I work on a few specific pieces: logos that are “almost there,” PDFs that are too long to revisit, photos that are nearly “profile ready,” and short clips that would land better with clean, readable captions.

Services

Where I can help.

Defined offerings that are easy to say yes to. You send what you have; I send back something more considered and ready to use.

Logo cleanup & refresh
Sharpen an existing logo, remove fuzziness, tidy proportions, and export clean files (including transparent PNGs). Light modern tweaks if you want it updated, not replaced.
Example: You send a screenshot logo from your old site → you receive a cleaned-up mark in multiple sizes, ready for business cards and social headers.
From $12 · $20–$25 with new concepts
Document & PDF summaries
Turn long PDFs, reports, or notes into structured bullet points and key ideas. Useful for study, planning, or sharing with a team.
Example: You send a 30-page industry report → you receive a 1–2 page summary with bullet points, definitions, and action items.
$10 per 10 pages
AI-enhanced headshots
Start from a decent photo and end up with professional-feeling headshots for LinkedIn, CVs, websites, and profiles—without booking a studio.
Example: You send 2–3 casual photos → you receive 2–3 polished headshots with refined lighting and background.
$10–$15 · 2–3 final options
Short-form captioning
Add clean, readable captions and subtle pacing to TikToks, Reels, or Shorts so they’re watchable on mute and easier to follow.
Example: You send a 30–45 sec vertical clip → you receive a captioned version with simple title frame and clear subtitles.
$10 per clip · 3 for $25
Business card layouts
Clean, legible layouts that pair with your logo and print well in both color and black & white.
Example: You send your logo and basic details → you receive a print-ready front/back card layout as a PDF + image.
$20 per layout · $30–$35 with logo
